Richard Bruce Cheney

A timeline of Richard Bruce Cheney's life

  1. 1941 - Born in Lincoln, Nebraska
  2. 1959 - Attended Yale, but later dropped out
  3. 1963 - Arrested twice for DWI, forcing him to dwell on his future
  4. 1964 - Married his high school sweetheart, Lynne Vincent
  5. 1966 - Obtained a master's degree in political science from the University of Wyoming
  6. 1989 - Served as Secretary of Defense until 1993
  7. 2001 - Served as Vice President for consecutive terms
  8. 2011 - Published his memoir, In My Time: A Personal and Political Memoir
